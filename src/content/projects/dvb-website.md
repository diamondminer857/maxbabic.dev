A multi-page static reference site covering digital television broadcasting
in the Czech Republic — DVB-T, DVB-T2, DVB-S, DVB-C, and IPTV. Built for the
LOOÚ course.

It documents Czech multiplexes, broadcast frequencies, and the transition
from DVB-T to DVB-T2 that wrapped up a few years ago. Plain HTML and CSS,
no frameworks.
