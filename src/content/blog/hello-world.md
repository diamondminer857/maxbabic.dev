This is the obligatory first post — partly to test that the blog actually works,
partly to put something here other than placeholder text.

## Why this site

I've had a personal domain for a while, but the site itself was a single page
with a few buttons linking to projects on GitHub. It worked, but it never really
went anywhere.

Now that the bachelor's thesis is done, I figured it was a good time to expand
this into something I might actually keep adding to. A blog, a project archive,
and a basic about page. Nothing fancy. Just a place to put things.

## What you'll find here

Probably write-ups about projects I'm working on (school or otherwise), notes
from the home server, occasional rants about cars or RF, and the kind of
half-finished posts that other people would gatekeep behind "draft" status
forever. We'll see.

If you're reading this — thanks for stopping by.
